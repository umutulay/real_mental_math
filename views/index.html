Welcome to Mental Math Exercises







Easy questions have two integers in the range [0,9] (except division)

Medium questions have one integer in the range [0,9] and one integer in the range [0,99] (except division)

Hard questions have two integers in the range [0,99] (except division)

Division answer is in the range [1,9] for easy, [1,30] for medium, [1,99] for hard
