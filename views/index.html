Welcome to Mental Math Exercises

Easy questions contain two integers in the range [0,9] (except division)

Medium questions contain one integer in the range [0,9] and one integer in the range [0,99] (except division)

Hard questions contain two integers in the range [0,99] (except division)

Division answer is in the range [1,9] for easy, [1,30] for medium, [1,99] for hard
